LOOPING WRANGLERS

GMTK 2025 - Loop



Instructions:

In this game you play as the cowboy. Your goal is to wrangle in as many cattle back into the pen as you can using your lasso (Loop). Why? Because each night your cattle mysteriously escape the pen and become targets of the hungry surrounding wolves. The longevity of your farm relies on you maintaining at least 10 cattle. How many days can you keep your farm alive? Be careful, the longer it takes to save your cattle each day, the more of a chance you give the wolves to call their friends.

Right Arrow: Turn Cowboy Right

Left Arrow: Turn Cowboy Left

Space Bar: Hold To Add Power, Release To Throw Lasso
